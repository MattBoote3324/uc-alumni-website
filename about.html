Our Mission

To build strong relationships and networks with University of Canterbury alumni and friends who can make a difference for our university.

What We Do

UC is proud of its alumni and friends and we honour the achievements and accomplishments of the men and women who have studied at the university. Building enduring relationships with our alumni and friends is vital for the future of UC. We are committed to keeping alumni and friends connected to the university and to each other.

Our Key Activites

  • Communicating with and helping alumni keep in touch with UC
  • Helping alumni build networks and connect with each other
  • Fostering philanthropic and other support for the university
  • Acknowledging and recognising donors and supporters

Who do I contact to join the Alumni Association?

As a UC Graduate, you are automatically one of our Alumni. There is no need to join however we would like you to let us know your current postal and email address.

How do I update my address details on the UC Alumni Database?

You can send us your address update by email: alumni@canterbury.ac.nz

My UC friends get emails about events in their area however I don’t. How can I get updates about events?

You need to ensure that we have your current address and email by emailing us at: alumni@canterbury.ac.nz
