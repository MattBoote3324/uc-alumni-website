Alumni Networks

Wherever in the world you may find yourself, you will find fellow UC alumni living and working near you. Our goal is to stay connected with you and help you connect with fellow alumni, wherever you are. You can join over 2000 other alumni on our UC alumni LinkedIn Group to extend your professional network globally.

If you would like to update your contact details, or are interested in starting up a network in your area please email alumni@canterbury.ac.nz.

University Bookshop (UBS)

UBS Canterbury is the home of all University of Canterbury merchandise, from Graduation Teddy Bears to UCan T Shirts.

You can purchase these gifts online and at Graduation ceremonies. You can also purchase these items on-campus at the University Bookshop

Graduate photography
