Why Attend an Alumni Event?

Attending alumni events is a great way to network with other UC alumni in your region and to find out the latest news about the university. Recently moved to a new area? Attending one of our events can help you meet new people who share similar backgrounds and form new friendships.

San Francisco Alumni Function

University of Canterbury Vice-Chancellor Dr Rod Carr, warmly invites you to attend our alumni function in San Francisco. This will be a great opportunity to catch up with other UC alumni and friends, and to keep in touch with your University.

When: Friday 30th September 2016

Time: 6.30pm - 8.30pm

Where: The Diplomat Club, Fairmont San Francisco, Atop Nob Hill, 950 Mason Street

Cost: Free

Other Information: You are welcome to bring a guest, please register them at the same time as your own registration.

Washington D.C. Alumni Function

University of Canterbury Chancellor Dr John Wood, together with the New Zealand Ambassador to the USA, H.E.The Hon Tim Groser would like to invite you to a University of Canterbury alumni reception at the Ambassador's Residence. This will be a great opportunity to catch up with other UC alumni and friends, and to keep in touch with your University.

When: Thursday 6th October 2016

Time: 6.30pm - 8.30pm

Where: New Zealand Ambassador's Residence, 27 Observatory Circle NW, Washington DC 20008

Cost: Free
